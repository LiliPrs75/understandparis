§ V — FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need before you arrive.

How far in advance should I book?

As soon as possible — especially peak season (spring and summer). That said, I do my best to accommodate last-minute requests.

Sessions in English or French?

Both! I work fluently in English and French, so we go with whatever feels most comfortable for you.

What happens after our session?

For video calls, you receive a written summary and resource sheet. For in-person sessions, I'm available for follow-up questions afterwards.

Can I book more than one session?

Absolutely. Many clients book a video call before their trip and an in-person session once they arrive — it's a great combination.

I've already planned everything — can you still help?

Yes — I offer itinerary reviews too. I'll spot the gaps and help you make it even better.

Do you work with families and groups?

Of course. I adapt every session — solo, couple, family with kids, friends, business travellers — all welcome.

What values guide your work?

Supporting local, independent businesses — neighbourhood bistros, independent boutiques, artisan makers. Proudly eco-conscious too.

How does payment work?

No upfront payment — settle via PayPal after your session, and your PDF recap follows immediately.

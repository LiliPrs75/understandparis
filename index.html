Questions

Frequently asked

How far in advance should I book?
+
As soon as possible — especially during peak season (spring and summer). That said, I do my best to accommodate last-minute requests, so don't hesitate to reach out even if your trip is coming up soon.
Are sessions in English or French?
+
Both! I work fluently in English and French, so we can go with whatever feels most comfortable for you.
What happens after our session?
+
For video call sessions, you'll receive a written summary and a resource sheet so you have everything in hand before you land. For in-person sessions, I'm available for follow-up questions afterwards.
Can I book more than one session?
+
Absolutely. Many clients book a video call before their trip and an in-person session once they arrive — it's a great combination.
What if I've already planned everything?
+
I offer itinerary reviews too. If you've already done your research and just want a second opinion from a local, I'll spot the gaps and help you make it even better.
How does payment work?
+
No upfront payment needed — just settle via PayPal whenever you're ready after our session, and your PDF recap will be on its way.
What if I need to reschedule or cancel?
+
Life happens — if you need to cancel or reschedule, just let me know at least 24 hours before our session and we'll find another time that works for you.
